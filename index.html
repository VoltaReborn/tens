Rules

Goal

Try to be the first player to get rid of every card: your hand cards, your face-up table cards, and finally your face-down table cards.

Taking a turn

On your turn, play a card that is the same value as or lower than the live pile card. If the pile is empty, you can play anything.

You may play multiple cards of the same rank at once. If you tap a card and have more of that same rank, the game can either auto-play the copies or ask how many to play, depending on your settings.

Your table cards

Each table stack starts with a face-up card over a face-down card. You can play from your hand or from any face-up table card. A face-down card becomes available only after the face-up card above it is gone.

Clearing the pile

A 10 clears the pile immediately. Four or more cards of the same rank in a row also clear the pile. When you clear, the pile disappears and you keep the turn.

Overplaying

If you play a card that is higher than the live pile card, that is an overplay. You must pick up the pile, including the card or cards you just overplayed, and then you immediately make another play.

Face-down flips

When you flip a face-down card, it must be played. If it matches other visible cards you have, it can be combined with them the same way any normal play can.

Scoring

When someone goes out, everyone else scores points for the cards they still have. Lower score is better.
